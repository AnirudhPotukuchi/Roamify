Culinary Delights

Kachori Sabzi and Tamatar Chaat

Varanasi's iconic street food centers on Kachori Sabzi (spiced lentil-filled bread with potato curry) and Tamatar Chaat (tangy, mashed tomato-based savory snack).

Lakshadweep Halwa

Lakshadweep Halwa, also known as Dweep Halwa or Dweep Unda, is a traditional, soft, and chewy sweet made primarily from coconut and palm jaggery.

Kashmiri Pulao

Kashmiri Pulao is a rich, mildly sweet, and aromatic Indian rice dish from Kashmiri cuisine, distinguished by its use of fragrant basmati rice, saffron, nuts, dried fruits, and fresh fruits.

North Karnataka Thali

A North Karnataka Thali in Hampi is a vibrant, spice-forward vegetarian meal centered on Jolada Rotti (sorghum flatbread) and Yenne Badanekayi (spicy stuffed brinjal curry).

Pyaaz Kachori

Pyaaz Kachori is an iconic, deep-fried Rajasthani snack famous in Jaipur. It consists of a crispy, flaky, golden-brown maida (refined flour) pastry stuffed with a spicy, savory filling of onions, potatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices (often including fennel, coriander, garlic, and chili).

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is an iconic Mumbai street food, often called the "Indian burger" or "Bombay burger". It consists of a deep-fried, spiced potato patty (batata vada) placed inside a soft bread roll (pav), typically accompanied by spicy garlic, green, or sweet chutneys, fried green chilies, and sometimes chopped onions.
